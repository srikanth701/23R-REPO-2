cooking

Cooking is the art of preparing food for consumption, typically by applying heat. It's a cultural universal that's been practiced by humans for thousands of years.

coding

Coding tells a machine which actions to perform and how to complete tasks. Programming languages provide the rules for building websites, apps, and other computer-based technologies.

cycling

Cycling, also known as bicycling or biking, is the activity of riding a bicycle or other type of cycle. It encompasses the use of human-powered vehicles .
